Art

Art is one of those thing's that I'm always _thinking_ about getting better at / doing, but I'm rarely ever creating anything in practice.

In terms of style, I'm really digging the aliased style (where you can see the pixels) of media such as Homestuck (yep)! I tend to draw small, square pieces (650x650 has been my spot), with pixel brushes to try to emphasize that.

I've been using Krita for drawing, and the default PixelArt 16 palette has been proving pretty motivating; having such a restricted (and at times unideal) palette of colors to choose from really relieves the burden of choice and makes everything _soo much easier_. Perhaps I'll be making more art more quickly in the coming future (keep tabs on my tumblr for more punctual updates on the matter.
